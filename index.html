Steps to make Vegetable Salad

  • Wash green beans, carrot, cabbage leaves, broccoli, capsicum, tomato and green chilies under running water.
  • Chop them and place in a steamer.
  • Steam for 5-7 minutes till they turn tender.
  • Once the vegetables look soft, transfer them to a large serving plate and drizzle lemon juice over it.
  • Your Mixed Vegetable Salad is now ready. Garnish them with coriander leaves and serve.
